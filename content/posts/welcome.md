Summary: Why I'm writing here, what you can expect — a short opening note.

I'm Volkan. Istanbul-based, mathematics background, Senior Data Scientist & AI Engineer. By day I work on Generative AI, MLOps and large-scale AIOps problems at [Logo Yazılım](https://www.logo.com.tr/). In the evenings I try to put what I learn — what I try, what I get stuck on, what catches my attention — into writing.

That's what this blog is for.

## What I'll write about

I'm not promising a single topic. There's a perspective rather than a theme: **whatever I'm learning as an engineer trying to build AI systems that run in production.**

Topics on my mind right now:

- **Generative AI & LLMs** — RAG systems, agents, text-to-SQL, the engineering side of prompt design
- **MLOps** — the bridges from prototype to production, pipeline design, observability
- **Software engineering with AI** — vibe coding, agentic engineering, what AI assistants can and can't actually do
- **Data & systems** — time-series, anomaly detection, OCR, computer vision pipelines

I might also write about something not on this list. Anything engineering that strikes me as interesting is fair game.

## Format

Short-to-medium, personal, experience-driven posts. Not "AI 101" style overviews — the walls I hit while trying something, the conclusions I reach, the open questions left in my head.

Some posts will be in Turkish, some in English — depends on the audience I'm writing for. The 3-part series on vibe coding and agents is in Turkish; this kind of opening note feels more natural in English.

If something here resonates, feel free to reach out on [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/volkanncicek) or [Twitter](https://twitter.com/volkanncicek).

Happy reading.
